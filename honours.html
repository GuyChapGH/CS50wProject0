Club Honours

Derby County FC has won four major honours. These are listed below.

  1. FA Cup Winner, 1945-46
  2. League Division One Champion, 1971-72
  3. League Division One Champion, 1974-75
  4. FA Charity Shield Winner, 1975

The club has also been runner-up several times in important competitions. In the early years between 1895-1902, Derby was FA Cup Runner-up three times and League Division One Runner-up once. In the 1930s the Rams were League Division One Runners-up twice. The first major win for the Club was the FA Cup in the post-war year. The 1970s were particularly memorable for Derby supporters being crowned League Division One Champions twice, winning the FA Charity Shield and playing in European competitions.
