Rivals

Table of League Wins, Draws and Losses against Nottingham Forest FC betweeen 1892-1994.

League Wins Draws Losses
Home 11 6 14
Away 10 6 15
Total 21 12 29

For Derby County, the East Midlands 'derby' match is with near neighbours Nottingham Forest, 16 miles to the East. The very first home game was in the season 1892-93 where Derby lost 3-2. The away fixture was no better for Derby where they lost 1-0. Attendance was around 10,000 for both of theses games. The peak attendance, between 1892-1994, was 42,000 people for the fixture at Nottingham Forest City Ground in the Season 1969-70 which Derby won 3-1.
