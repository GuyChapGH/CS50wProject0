History


This is NOT Pride Park (current home of DCFC). Photo by Thomas Serer on Unsplash.

Derby County FC was founded in 1884 by the Derbyshire County Cricket Club. They became one of the founder members of the Football League when it was established in 1888. They are known as the Rams, a historical link between Derbyshire and the woollen industry.

The club has played at three grounds in Derby. These are:

  • County Ground, 1884-1894
  • Baseball Ground, 1895 - 1996
  • Pride Park, 1997 - present
